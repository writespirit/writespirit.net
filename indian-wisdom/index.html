Welcome to AspiringIndia.org


India is not just a place.
India is not just a people.
India is the celestial music,
And inside this music
Anybody from any corner of the globe
 Can find the real significance of life.

                             Sri Chinmoy

India has always been the land of ever-renewing aspiration, from the time of the ancient seers and their lofty utterances of truth, to the revitalizing messages of the spiritual teachers of today. At AspiringIndia.org we aim to give you a taste of the vast spiritual legacy India has left for mankind.

 

Here at AspiringIndia.org we have:

  • Extracts from and commentaries on the great Indian scriptures: the Vedas and the Upanishads.
  • The Bhagavad Gita: full translation and illumining commentaries.
  • New: Yoga section describing the three paths to enlightenment that have existed since time immemorial: bhakti yoga, jnana yoga and karma yoga.
  • Understanding the Darshanas:Information on the six great schools of Indian philosophy: Nyaya, Vaiseseka, Samkhya, Patanjali’s Yoga, Purva Mimamsa, and Vedanta.
  • A look at Hinduism and its many customs and traditions

  • Information on Indian saints, sages, and spiritual Masterssuch as:

  • Sri Sankaracharya
  • Sri Ramakrishna
  • Swami Brahmananda
  • Dayananda Saraswati
  • Anandamayi Ma            
  • Sri Sarada Devi
  • Ramana Maharshi
  • Sri Chinmoy

Insofar as possible, our commentaries are sourced from the writings of teachers with living experience of the Truth these texts embody. We are adding new material to the site all the time – we soon hope to have sections on the contributions of Buddhism, Sikhism, Jainism and Sufism/Islam to the vast reservoir of Indian spirituality.

 AspiringIndia.org is designed and mantained by members of the Sri Chinmoy Centre. We hope you enjoy your visit.
